About Me

Hello! My name is Tara Boland and I am a graphic designer, studying at Algonquin College. I’m an optimistic, hard-working student with a love for all things design-related. My specialties include brochures and booklets, logo designs and posters.

On this website, you will find some of my highlighted portfolio pieces as well as my contact information.

Portfolio

Postcard Advertisement

This is a postcard advertisement designed for a company called Flexi Lexi. Flexi Lexi promotes healthy eating habits as well as juicing. The goal in this postcard is to promote these things in a fun and fresh way. I created small fruits using the same style of that in the Flexi Lexi logo. I kept the colours vibrant so that the viewer may see this company as joyful and fun.

Book Cover & Dust Jacket

This is a book cover and full dust jacket designed for the novel Fifth Business by Robertson Davies. After doing extensive research, as well as reading the novel, I decided to base this design one simple theme that is seen at the beginning of the novel, and returns in the end – the stone. I created a simplistic, clean design to reflect the novel.

Rolling Stone Spread

This is a Rolling Stone Magazine spread mock-up about rapper, Nicki Minaj. I wanted the spread to reflect Nicki Minaj’s persona and being, and therefore added pops of pink across the spread. I used the grid in many different ways to create a unique feel. Nicki Minaj is an independent woman who stands out and I reflected this in my spread by keeping photos to a minimum, with one large image creating a high-impact focal point.

Information Booklet

This is an information booklet created for the WWF (World Wildlife Foundation). I used colours provided by the WWF to create a relation between the booklet and the company. I kept the layout and fonts looking clean and professional, but added a script font to the headings to give the booklet a more personalized feel.

Brochure

This is a brochure, created for HappyCow, to help spread information and awareness on veganism. I used the colour green throughout the layout to represent plants, health, and a more environmentally-friendly planet. I also used a purple to help reflect HappyCow (their logo if a purple cow).

Celebrity Illustration

This is an illustration of Alice Cooper, rock-and-roll legend, created in a style inspired by that of artist, Pablo Lobato. The goal in this piece was to create a recognizable celebrity using simple 2-D shapes, as well as other specific techniques. I used Adobe Illustrator to create this piece.
